Our Story

We are committed to making our story about you and your successful journey, serving as an insightful and resourceful partner.

Your partners in sustainable change

Reubro has always done things differently. Founded by commercially-trained pilot Philip Abraham, we were among the first to pull the principles of aviation into a business context. The business was built around those principles - experiential learning, a human-centred approach, data-driven decision making and a culture of fairness above all.

A human approach to business improvement

As it turns out, the framework that keeps people safe in the air is just as valuable when it comes to running great companies. We grew our reputation on that innovative basis, building a strong client base Kochi.  Over 40 years later, we’ve expanded our reach, but we go back to those original principles all the time. Times may have changed, but in today’s complex, fast-moving world, we’re still looking for the simplest, smartest way forward.

Our Purpose and Values
Our purpose

Our purpose is quite simple: to enable people to work in a simpler, smarter way. This has never been more relevant, in a world where the speed of digital transformation is disrupting entire industries and changing competitive landscapes.

We are a values-based organisation

Our values are embedded in everything we do. Sysdoc people are Awesome Together. We work with High Energy and Absolute Integrity. We Celebrate Simplicity and have a Relentless Commitment to get the right results for our clients, and each other.
